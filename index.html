Fall Inspo!

Clothes

  • Chunky sweaters-fall colors, or any type
  • Boots-can be worn with skirts, or over skinny jeans to create a vintage look
  • Uggs, or any other type of furry boot!
  • Vintage leather bags
  • Colors-brown,yellow,beige,orange
  • Leg warmers!

Actvities

There are so many fun activities to do in fall! Once autumn approaches, there are many fun things to do with family and friends. One example is pumpkin picking! Visiting a pumpkin patch with family and friends can be so fun, and after picking out pumpkins, there are many options relating to what to do with them, such as making pumpkin soup or pie, or decorating or carving the pumpkins. As well as this, other activities include visiting haunted houses, going trick or treating, and picking out a halloween costume!
